Landowner Services

Whether you're preparing to build, clear a lot, or simply want to maximize your property’s potential, Elite Earthworks is here to help. We provide landowners across Southern California with expert grading, clearing, and site prep services—done right, from the ground up.

  • Weed Abatement

    Managing large acreage requires specialized weed abatement solutions. We offer comprehensive services tailored to the unique needs of large parcels, including mowing, disking, brush clearing, and vegetation management. Our experienced team utilizes specialized equipment to efficiently and effectively control weeds, reducing fire risk and promoting a healthy landscape.

  • Land Clearing & Grading

    Our land clearing and grading services provide comprehensive solutions for preparing your property for construction or landscaping. We handle tree removal, brush clearing, stump grinding, excavation, grading, and site leveling, ensuring a smooth and accurate foundation for your project.

  • Drainage Repairs

    With specialized equipment and years of experience, we provide expert drainage and washout repair services for large acre parcels. Our team understands the unique challenges of managing extensive properties and delivers efficient, cost-effective solutions.

  • Slope Reconstruction

    We specialize in excavation, grading, and site leveling to create a stable and accurate foundation for your project. Whether you need to reshape terrain, improve drainage, or reinforce slopes, our expert team ensures precise and effective slope reconstruction solutions.

  • Drone Topography

    Our drone topography services provide accurate mapping and detailed 3D models for construction, land development, and site planning. We also offer high-definition video for marketing and inspections, as well as stunning aerial photography for real estate, events, and more. With experienced pilots and state-of-the-art equipment, we deliver precise, dependable results every time.

  • Fire Control

    Proactive fire prevention is crucial for large acreage properties. Our land clearing services create defensible space, reducing the fuel load and slowing the spread of wildfires. Protect your investment and the surrounding environment with our professional fire control solutions.
